Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Mastermind is developed by Colegio Solutions s.r.o. ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application handles information.

The short version: Mastermind collects only the data you explicitly provide (your nickname and game scores) for the global leaderboard. No tracking, no ads, no analytics.

Data We Collect

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your game statistics (games played, wins, best score)
  • Your in-app purchase status — verified and managed by Apple's StoreKit
  • Your difficulty preferences
  • Your chosen nickname

The following data is sent to our server (colegio.cz) if you choose to participate in the global leaderboard:

  • Your nickname (chosen by you)
  • Your score, difficulty level, number of guesses, and time taken

Participation in the leaderboard is entirely optional. You can skip nickname setup and play without sending any data to our server.

How We Use Your Data

Leaderboard data is used solely to display global rankings within the app and on colegio.cz. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

Data Storage

Leaderboard data is stored on servers operated by Active24 s.r.o. in the Czech Republic (EU). No data is transferred outside the European Union.

In-App Purchases

Mastermind offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Mastermind Pro"). All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any personal data related to your purchase.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Mastermind does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the new version will be posted here. As long as the app remains offline and data-free, this policy will not meaningfully change.
