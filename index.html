About Colegio Solutions

A small software company based near Prague, Czech Republic.

Who we are

Colegio Solutions s.r.o. is a Czech software company founded in Sibřina, a small village east of Prague. We build focused, well-crafted applications — tools and games that are simple to use and enjoyable to interact with.

Mastermind is our first iOS game, built from the ground up with SwiftUI and a deep respect for the original board game that has challenged minds since 1970.

Our values

  • Simplicity — no clutter, no dark patterns, no subscriptions.
  • Accessibility — colorblind-safe design, localised in 22 languages.
  • Fair pricing — one small payment unlocks everything. Forever.
  • Quality — native SwiftUI, no third-party SDKs, no ads.
